Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

How do I enroll in MILMICH FX ACADEMY?

+

Enrollment is simple! Click the WhatsApp button or fill out the contact form above. Our admissions team will guide you through the course selection process and get you started within 24 hours. We offer both online and in-person classes.

Do I need any prior trading experience?

+

Not at all. Our beginner courses are designed for absolute newcomers. We start from the basics — what forex is, how markets work, and how to read charts — before moving to advanced strategies. Many of our most successful students started with zero knowledge.

What is included in the Premium Signal Service?

+

Our Premium Signal Service delivers high-probability trade setups directly to your phone via WhatsApp and Telegram. You receive entry points, stop-loss levels, take-profit targets, and detailed analysis for forex pairs, crypto, and binary options — with a 94%+ win rate.

Can I learn from outside Nigeria?

+

Absolutely! We have students from Ghana, South Africa, the UK, Canada, and the USA. All our courses are available online via live Zoom sessions, recorded video modules, and our private community group. Time zone flexibility is built into our schedule.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept Bank Transfer, Mobile Money, Paystack, and Cryptocurrency (BTC, USDT). For international students, we also accept PayPal and Wise transfers. Payment plans are available for our comprehensive mentorship packages.
